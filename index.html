How would you define poverty?

The term “poverty” refers to a financial state. Beyond income, it encompasses lack of power, representation, and liberty. Poor people lack the necessities of life and have limited opportunities. Poverty affects children physically, socially, emotionally, and spiritually.

Impacts of poverty on children

Poverty affects children throughout their lives, causing physical, emotional, and social deprivation. Poverty increases rates of child mortality, lowers life expectancy, and reduces educational achievements.
